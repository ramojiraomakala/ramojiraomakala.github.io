DP Fine-Tuning of LLMs (LLM_Tune)

2025

A framework for fine-tuning large pre-trained models (DeBERTa-v3-large, ViT-base, Qwen2.5-0.5B) under formal differential privacy. My Low-Rank Reparametrization (LRK) decomposes each weight matrix into rank-1 carriers and noises only those under DP-SGD — shrinking the noised parameter count ~500× and holding accuracy within 2–4% of non-private baselines (88% SST-2, 97% CIFAR-10, 90% medical) across 8 datasets.

Federated Fine-Tuning of LLMs with DP

2025

A research framework that fine-tunes Qwen 2.5, Llama 3.2, Phi-3 and BERT across simulated clients with the Flower framework on GLUE / SuperGLUE. Only LoRA's ~0.1% trainable params are exchanged per round — ~1000× less bandwidth and a tractable DP budget. A full client × round sweep on Qwen-0.5B showed LoRA matching or beating full fine-tuning by ~4 pts on QNLI/MNLI. Built for HPC reality: SLURM checkpoint/resume, per-job isolation, and Ray-actor caches. Phase 1 toward a multi-mechanism DP study.
